About this Dataset.¶

Dataset Description :¶
  • Product_ID: Unique identifier for the product.
  • Batch_ID: Unique identifier for the batch.
  • Manufacturing_Date: Date when the product was manufactured.
  • Material_Code: Code for the material used.
  • Machine_ID: Identifier for the machine used in manufacturing.
  • Shift: Shift during which the manufacturing took place (e.g., morning, evening, night).
  • Operator_ID: Identifier for the operator handling the machine.
  • Production_Time: Time taken for manufacturing (in hours).
  • Defect_Flag: Whether the product was defective (0 for no, 1 for yes).
  • Quality_Score: Score indicating the quality of the product.
  • Temperature: Temperature during the manufacturing process (in Celsius).
  • Pressure: Pressure during the manufacturing process (in PSI).
  • Humidity: Humidity during the manufacturing process (in %).
  • Vibration_Level: Vibration level of the machine (in mm/s).
  • Maintenance_Flag: Whether the machine required maintenance after the production (0 for no, 1 for yes).
  • Downtime: The amount of time the machine was down during production.
  • Energy_Consumption: The amount of energy consumed during production.
  • Cycle_Time: The time taken to complete one production cycle.
  • Scrap_Rate: The percentage of material wasted during production.
  • Operator_Experience: The years of experience of the operator.
  • Temperature_Deviation: The deviation from the target temperature.
  • Pressure_Deviation: The deviation from the target pressure.

# Splitting the dataset

# Function to plot overfitting and underfitting
def plot_overfitting_underfitting(X_train, y_train, X_test, y_test):
    train_accuracies = []
    test_accuracies = []
    max_depths = list(range(1, 21))
    
    for max_depth in max_depths:
        model = XGBClassifier(max_depth=max_depth, use_label_encoder=False, eval_metric='logloss', random_state=42)
        model.fit(X_train, y_train)
        
        y_train_pred = model.predict(X_train)
        y_test_pred = model.predict(X_test)
        
        train_accuracies.append(accuracy_score(y_train, y_train_pred))
        test_accuracies.append(accuracy_score(y_test, y_test_pred))
    
    plt.figure(figsize=(10, 6))
    plt.plot(max_depths, train_accuracies, label='Training accuracy', marker='o')
    plt.plot(max_depths, test_accuracies, label='Validation accuracy', marker='o')
    plt.xlabel('Max depth of XGBClassifier')
    plt.ylabel('Accuracy')
    plt.title('Overfitting and Underfitting in XGBClassifier')
    plt.legend()
    plt.show()

# Plotting
plot_overfitting_underfitting(X_train, y_train, X_test, y_test)
